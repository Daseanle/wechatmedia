Flux.1 vs Midjourney v6: Is the New Open-Source King Better?

For the past two years, Midjourney has been the undisputed king of AI art. But a new challenger has arrived: Flux.1 by Black Forest Labs (created by the original Stable Diffusion team).

WeChat media creators are asking: "Can I finally cancel my Midjourney subscription?" Let's find out.

Head-to-Head Comparison

1. Text Rendering (Typography)

Can it write "WeChat" correctly on a signboard?

üèÜ Winner: Flux.1 (By a mile)

Flux can render long sentences almost perfectly. Midjourney v6 still struggles with complex text.

2. Photorealism & Hands

ü§ù Tie (It depends)

Flux is more "real" (skin texture looks like a raw photo). Midjourney is more "cinematic" and artistic.

3. Ease of Use

How hard is it to start prompting?

üèÜ Winner: Midjourney

Despite the Discord interface, Midjourney requires less hardware knowledge. Flux needs a strong GPU or a cloud host (like Replicate/FAL).

Final Verdict for Creators

  • Switch to Flux.1 if: You need to generate posters with text, you want to run AI on your own computer (save money), or you prefer uncensored local generation.
  • Stick with Midjourney if: You are an art director who needs specific artistic styles, cinematic lighting, and doesn't want to deal with technical installations.
